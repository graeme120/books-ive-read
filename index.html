Books I've Read, 2020 Onwards

In early 2020 I had an ephiphany regarding the beauty of knowledge, and what a privilege it is to be able to educate oneself. Suddenly, school, books, classes, all forms of education acquired a weight that I hadn't previously considered. We have so much power to influence and elevate our own thinking, and have never had more access to resources to do so. Although this access to information comes with many caveats, the fact of its existence I still consider to be a marvellous feat by humanity.

Here are all the books i've read since that epiphany, complete with PDFs, so that my knowledge can become yours as well.
